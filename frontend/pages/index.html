How fast can a donation be picked up?

Most urban requests are matched within 30 to 60 minutes based on volunteer capacity and proximity. Our AI routing prioritizes critical, highly perishable items instantly.

What food types are accepted?

We accept freshly prepared meals, packaged surplus, baked goods, and raw produce. You can specify quantity, required storage conditions (e.g., refrigeration), and exact expiry times during listing.

Do volunteers receive route support?

Absolutely. Volunteers receive live map navigation, intelligently suggested multi-stop routes, drop-off sequence priorities, and contact details for seamless handoffs.

Can organizations use this for CSR reporting?

Yes. Every completed food rescue is permanently logged on your dashboard. Partner restaurants and corporations can instantly export compliance-ready impact metrics for CSR and tax purposes.

Is there any cost to use FoodBridge?

No. FoodBridge operates as a verified non-profit matching platform. It is 100% free for donors, volunteers, and verified NGOs.
